Q11 (Case Study)
Mom & Pop Café runs private EC2s with a Bastion Host already in place. They need the private EC2s to download updates from the internet. What should they add?
Best practice: SSH via Bastion; outbound internet from private subnet via NAT Gateway (not via Bastion).
